Install it (sideload)

  1. On your phone, tap Download APK above to get VoxTerm-android-arm64.apk.
  2. Open the downloaded file. Android will warn the source isn't allowed to install apps — tap Settings → Allow from this source (it's per-app, e.g. for your browser or Files app), then go back.
  3. Tap Install, then Open.
  4. On your first recording, grant the microphone permission.

Use it

  • Tap record, talk, then tap stop — your transcript appears and is saved in the app's history.
  • Everything runs on the phone: you can switch Wi-Fi and mobile data fully off.
  • Export your transcript to Markdown, JSON, or subtitles (SRT/VTT).

Stay updated automatically (optional)

Use Obtainium to auto-update straight from the source:

  1. Install Obtainium.
  2. Add App → paste https://github.com/dmarzzz/VoxTerm
  3. Pick the android-latest release / VoxTerm-android-arm64.apk, then Add → Install.

Every new build bumps the app's version, so Obtainium will offer the update on its own.

Good to know

  • Works on arm64 phones running Android 7.0+ (essentially every phone since ~2017).
  • The download is ~150–230 MB — it bundles the offline Whisper speech model.
  • On-device scope is record → transcript → export. Speaker diarization, AI-summarize, and system-audio capture are desktop-only.
