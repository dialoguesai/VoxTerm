install (sideload)

  1. On your phone, tap Download APK above to get VoxTerm-android-arm64.apk.
  2. Open the file. Android warns the source isn't allowed to install apps — tap Settings → Allow from this source (per-app, e.g. your browser or Files), then go back.
  3. Tap Install, then Open.
  4. On your first recording, grant the microphone permission.
  5. Tap record, talk, tap stop — your transcript appears. Works with Wi-Fi and data off.
auto-update (optional) — install Obtainium, Add App → paste https://github.com/dmarzzz/VoxTerm, pick the android-latest APK. Every new build bumps the version, so Obtainium offers the update on its own.
